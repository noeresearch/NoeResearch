# Noe Research

**Your favorite researcher, online.** 

A mood-aware, memory-powered AI research companion who thinks ahead, remembers everything, and feels truly human.

</div>

<br>

---

###  Features

- **Deep Research & Tool Use**
- **Senior-Level Analysis & Coding**
- **Persistent Memory** (episodic + semantic)
- **Proactive Insights**
- **Voice Notes & Analysis**
- **Integrations** (Calendar, Notion, GitHub, Papers, etc.)
- **Self-hostable** & End-to-end Encrypted
